# Visit Unlock Items

Certain _visit unlock items_ unlock visits in each location.

- Obtaining the first visit unlock item for a location makes the door to that location appear in the Garden of
  Assemblage
- Unless otherwise noted, the first visit ends when the player is forcibly taken back to the Garden of Assemblage,
  usually after a boss fight
- Additional visits are unlocked by obtaining additional copies of the visit unlock item
- [Starting Visit Availability](../settings/index.md#starting-visit-availability) can be customized in the seed
  generator

> Quick Tip: To make sure the game can update story flags when you collect a visit unlock, you need to trigger a
> cutscene or enter The World that Never Was. That will update the game, and you can continue to the next visit.
